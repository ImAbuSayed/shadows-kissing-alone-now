[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Shadows kissing on the cabin wall
Catch my breath every time you fall
Into my arms when the lights burn low
Nowhere else that we need to go

[Verse 1]
There's a quiet secret in the evening air
Tangled up inside your midnight hair
Tracing the miles that brought us right here
Drawing you close till the world disappears

[Pre-Chorus]
Every heartbeat echoes like a distant drum
Telling me you're the only one
I feel the heat of your skin against mine
Losing all track of the passing time

[Chorus]
We're just shadows kissing alone now
Finding our way through the dark somehow
No need to rush what we're feeling tonight
Holding onto you till the morning light

[Post-Chorus]
Just shadows kissing alone
Making this midnight our own

[Verse 2]
I can taste the sweetness on your tender lips
Following the rhythm of your fingertips
Leaving the ghosts of the past at the door
Giving you all that I am and more

[Pre-Chorus 2]

Telling me you're the only one

Losing all track of the passing time

[Chorus]

No need to rush what we're feeling tonight

[Post-Chorus 2]
Just shadows kissing alone
Making this midnight our own

[Bridge]
Look at the embers fading to gray
Sweeping the noise of the daylight away
Even the stars seem to hold in their breath
Caught in this beautiful, infinite depth
I'll give you everything, all that you need
Forever your shelter, forever Saeed

[Spoken]
I can feel your pulse racing right next to mine
Let's stay right here, suspended in time

[Final Chorus]
We're just shadows kissing alone now
Finding our way through the dark somehow
No need to rush what we're feeling tonight
Holding onto you till the morning light
